How to Sell Gold in Nepal: Get the Best Price in 2025

Step-by-step guide to selling gold in Nepal — how dealers calculate buyback price, what to bring, where to sell, and tips to maximise your return.

Guide

How Dealers Calculate Gold Buyback Price in Nepal

When you sell gold to a Nepal dealer, the price they offer is based on a simple formula:

Buyback = Weight × (Karat ÷ 24) × Today's FENEGOSIDA 24K Buying Rate

For example: 2 tola of 22K Tejabi gold sold today:
2 × (22/24) × FENEGOSIDA buying rate (usually NPR 1,000–2,000 below the selling rate)

💡 Key difference: FENEGOSIDA announces both a selling rate (what shops charge you) and a buying rate (what they pay you). The spread is typically NPR 1,000–2,500 per tola — this is the dealer's margin on your sale.

What Happens to Making Charges When You Sell?

You lose all making charges. When a dealer buys back jewellery, they calculate only the gold content value — not the craftsmanship you paid for. This is why financial advisors recommend not counting jewellery as pure investment gold. The making charges (8–18% of gold value) are a sunk cost.

Example: You bought a necklace for NPR 3,00,000 (gold NPR 2,58,000 + making NPR 42,000). When selling, you receive only the gold value: ~NPR 2,53,000 (using the buying rate). That's a loss of NPR 47,000 on the making charges plus spread.

Documents to Bring When Selling

  • Original purchase receipt (if available — helps confirm purity)
  • Citizenship certificate or photo ID
  • The gold item(s) — clean them if possible for accurate weighing

Tips to Get the Best Buyback Price

  • Check today's FENEGOSIDA buying rate before visiting any shop
  • Get quotes from at least 3 shops — buyback rates can vary by NPR 500–1,000/tola
  • Sell bars/coins separately from jewellery — bars command a better rate as there's no making charge loss
  • Avoid selling on festival days — shops are busy and may offer lower prices knowing you need quick cash
  • Know your purity — if you have a 916/22K hallmark receipt, you can negotiate from a position of certainty
