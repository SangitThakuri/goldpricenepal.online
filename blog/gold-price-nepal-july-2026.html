July 2026 Gold Price Summary

Nepal's gold price showed a gradual upward trend in July 2026, rising from approximately NPR 2,78,000 per tola at the start of the month to NPR 2,82,100 by July 20. The month was characterised by steady international gold demand and a slightly weaker NPR against the USD.

Key Drivers in July 2026

  • International gold: International spot ranged USD 3,980–4,020/oz during July. Relative stability with slight upward bias.
  • USD/NPR: The Rupee weakened slightly from NPR 153.8 to 154.3 against USD, adding to domestic gold cost.
  • Local demand: Pre-Shrawan wedding season and Shrawan Sombar (auspicious Mondays) boosted jewellery demand in the first half of the month.
  • Import volume: Normal import levels; no quota restrictions affecting supply.

📅 Shrawan effect: Shrawan (July–August in BS calendar) is an auspicious month with multiple Sombar (Monday) festivals when Hindus traditionally wear gold. This seasonally supports gold demand in Nepal.
