Best Auspicious Dates to Buy Gold in Nepal 2026 — Shubh Muhurat

When is the best time to buy gold in Nepal in 2026? Auspicious dates — Akshaya Tritiya, Dhanteras, Dashain, Tihar, Shrawan Sombar — and their significance for gold buying.

Why Do Auspicious Dates Matter for Gold Buying in Nepal?

In Nepal's Hindu and Buddhist traditions, buying gold on auspicious dates (शुभ मुहूर्त) is believed to bring prosperity and good fortune. This cultural belief drives significant seasonal demand spikes, which means prices can be slightly elevated — but the tradition remains deeply embedded in Nepali culture.

Key Gold Buying Dates in Nepal 2026

1. Dhanteras — October 18, 2026

Dhanteras (Dhan Trayodashi) is considered the single most auspicious day to buy gold across Nepal and India. Buying gold on this day is believed to bring Goddess Lakshmi's blessings of wealth. Gold shops across Nepal see their single busiest day of the year on Dhanteras.

⚠️ Price impact: Demand on Dhanteras can spike local premiums slightly. Consider buying 3–5 days before to get the same auspiciousness without the queue premium.

2. Shrawan Sombar (Mondays in Shrawan) — July & August 2026

The Shrawan month (mid-July to mid-August) is auspicious for Lord Shiva worship. Each Monday (Sombar) during Shrawan, devotees traditionally wear gold or buy new gold as an offering. Remaining Shrawan Sombars in 2026: July 21, July 28, August 4, August 11.

3. Dashain Tika — October 12, 2026

Dashain Tika is Nepal's biggest festival day. Families traditionally gift gold to daughters and sisters. Jewellers report high sales in the 2 weeks leading up to Tika.

4. Tihar (Deepawali) — October 19–23, 2026

The Tihar festival, especially Laxmi Puja (October 20) and Bhai Tika (October 23), sees heavy gold gifting between siblings and family members. The week of Tihar is Nepal's second highest gold sales period after Dashain-Dhanteras.

5. Vivah Panchami & Wedding Muhurtas — Nov–Dec 2026, Feb–May 2027

Wedding season is the largest driver of gold jewellery purchases. Key muhurat dates in 2026: November 15–30, December 1–15. In 2027: February 15 – May 20 (Baisakh-Jestha season).

6. Akshaya Tritiya — Expected April/May 2027

Akshaya Tritiya (Akha Teej) is considered a permanently auspicious day — any gold bought on this date is believed to multiply. It typically falls in late April or May. The 2027 date falls in late April.
