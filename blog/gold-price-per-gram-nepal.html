How Much Is 1 Gram of Gold in Nepal?

Most international gold prices are quoted per gram or per troy ounce, while Nepal's FENEGOSIDA uses tola (11.664 grams) as the standard unit. Here's how to convert between them.

Formula: Price per gram = FENEGOSIDA tola rate ÷ 11.664

Gram vs Tola: Which Unit to Use?

In Nepal, gold is traditionally sold and priced by tola. However, lighter jewellery items (thin chains, small pendants, nose pins) are often measured in grams or aana. When comparison shopping:

  • Always ask for the price per gram or per tola, not just total price
  • Convert to a common unit before comparing across shops
  • Making charges may be quoted as % of gold value or as flat NPR per gram — confirm which

Why Is Nepal's Price Per Gram Higher Than International?

The international gold spot price in USD converts to approximately NPR 18,000–19,000 per gram (at July 2026 rates). Nepal's retail price of NPR 24,186 per gram is about 27% higher due to:

  • 10% customs duty on gold imports
  • Agricultural development fee (0.5%)
  • FENEGOSIDA member margins
  • Retail dealer margin (varies)

How to Calculate Your Jewellery Cost by Gram

For a jewellery piece that weighs, say, 8 grams in 22K gold:

  1. Gold value = 8g × NPR 22,175 = NPR 1,77,400
  2. Making charges (say 12%) = NPR 1,77,400 × 0.12 = NPR 21,288
  3. VAT on making (13%) = NPR 21,288 × 0.13 = NPR 2,767
  4. Total = NPR 2,01,455
