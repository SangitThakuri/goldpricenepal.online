Editorial Policy

How we source, verify, and present gold and silver price information for Nepal

Gold Price Nepal is committed to providing accurate, timely, and transparent gold and silver price data to the public. This editorial policy explains how we work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be Nepal's most reliable and accessible source for gold and silver price information. We serve retail buyers, jewellers, investors, students, and anyone who needs to understand precious metal prices in Nepal.

Primary Data Source

All gold and silver prices displayed on this website come exclusively from FENEGOSIDA — the Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (नेपाल सुनचाँदी व्यवसायी महासंघ). FENEGOSIDA is the official body that sets daily gold and silver rates in Nepal.

  • Prices are updated each business day, typically by mid-morning (10:00–11:00 AM NST)
  • We do not alter, extrapolate, or adjust FENEGOSIDA rates
  • We clearly label all prices as coming from FENEGOSIDA
  • On bank holidays and weekends, the previous business day's rate remains in effect — we display this clearly

Price Display Standards

  • Currency: All prices are displayed in Nepali Rupees (NPR) unless explicitly stated otherwise
  • Units: Primary unit is per Tola (11.664 grams). We also display per gram and per 10 grams
  • Purity levels: We display rates for 24K (Fine Gold / Tejabi), 22K (Tejabi), 18K, 14K, and Silver (Chandi) as published by FENEGOSIDA
  • Timestamps: Every price display includes a last-updated timestamp so users know how current the data is

Calculators and Tools

Our Gold Calculator, Profit Calculator, Loan Calculator, and other tools use live FENEGOSIDA prices as their base rate. All calculator results are:

  • Clearly labeled as estimates or indicative figures
  • Not a substitute for quotes from licensed dealers or banks
  • Recalculated each time a new price update is received

Editorial Independence

  • We do not accept payment to alter, promote, or suppress any price information
  • We do not accept paid placements that are presented as editorial content
  • Any advertising or sponsored content is clearly labeled as such
  • Our editorial decisions are not influenced by advertisers or commercial partners

Corrections Policy

If we publish incorrect information, we correct it promptly. We:

  • Update the affected page immediately when an error is identified
  • Do not quietly delete or alter pages to hide errors
  • Welcome corrections from users — email us at the contact page

What We Do Not Cover

  • Real-time live streaming prices (our prices are updated once daily per FENEGOSIDA)
  • International gold spot prices in USD (available on international commodities sites)
  • Individual dealer or jeweller prices (these vary; our prices are the FENEGOSIDA reference rate)
  • Financial advice or investment recommendations

Use of Artificial Intelligence

We use AI tools to assist with content drafting, translation, and summarisation. All AI-drafted content is reviewed by our editorial team for accuracy before publication. Prices and data are always sourced directly from FENEGOSIDA — AI is never used to generate or estimate prices.
