How to Use the Gold Calculator — Nepal

Select a calculator tab above:

  • Gold Value: Enter weight in tola, aana, lal, or grams. Select purity (24K, 22K, 18K, 14K, or Silver). Adjust making charges slider for jewellery total.
  • Profit / Loss: Enter your original buy price, current price, weight, and making charges paid. Instantly see your profit or loss in NPR and %.
  • Gold Loan: Enter weight and purity, set your expected LTV ratio (65–80%), and see the estimated loan amount banks will offer against your gold.
  • Making Charges: Full jewellery price breakdown — gold value + making charge % + VAT on making charges.
  • Savings Goal: Set a target gold weight and timeline — see how much to save per month and per day to reach your goal.

Gold Weight Units in Nepal

  • 1 Tola (तोला) = 11.664 grams = 16 Aana
  • 1 Aana (आना) = 0.7290 grams = 10 Lal
  • 1 Lal (लाल) = 0.0729 grams
  • 1 Gram = 1.3717 Aana = 0.08573 Tola
