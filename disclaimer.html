2. Price Accuracy

Our gold prices are calculated using:

  • International gold spot prices (USD per troy ounce) from third-party market data providers
  • Live USD to NPR exchange rates
  • Estimated Nepal import duties (customs duty ~10%) and VAT (13%)
  • Estimated dealer margins

While we strive for accuracy, these calculations are approximations. Actual retail prices in Nepal depend on:

  • Official rates announced daily by FENEGOSIDA
  • Individual dealer pricing and margins
  • Current exchange rate at the time of transaction
  • Government policy changes affecting duties or taxes
  • Local supply and demand conditions

3. Official Nepal Gold Rates

The official gold and silver rates in Nepal are set each morning by the Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (FENEGOSIDA). We strongly recommend consulting FENEGOSIDA's daily rate announcement or a registered Nepal gold dealer before making any purchase or sale.
