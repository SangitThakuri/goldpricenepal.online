Data Sources

Full transparency on how we collect, process, and display Nepal gold and silver prices

Primary Source: FENEGOSIDA

All gold and silver prices on this website are sourced from FENEGOSIDA — the Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association (नेपाल सुनचाँदी व्यवसायी महासंघ).

FENEGOSIDA is the authoritative body responsible for setting and publishing official daily gold and silver rates in Nepal. All licenced gold dealers and jewellers in Nepal are required to follow FENEGOSIDA rates.

  • FENEGOSIDA website: fenegosida.com.np
  • Price announcement: Rates are typically announced each business day by 10:00–11:00 AM NST
  • Applicable to: All gold and silver transactions at licensed dealers throughout Nepal

How Prices Are Set in Nepal

Nepal's gold prices are influenced by several factors that FENEGOSIDA considers when setting daily rates:

  • International spot price: The global gold spot price (USD per troy ounce), quoted on international commodity exchanges
  • USD/NPR exchange rate: The Nepal Rastra Bank daily exchange rate, since gold is imported and priced internationally in USD
  • Import duty and taxes: Nepal levies customs duty on gold imports; any changes to duty rates affect the domestic price
  • Market supply and demand: Seasonal demand (wedding season, Teej, Dashain) and domestic supply conditions

FENEGOSIDA synthesises these factors and publishes a single unified rate that member dealers must follow. We report this rate exactly as published — we do not calculate or adjust it ourselves.

Price Update Pipeline

  1. FENEGOSIDA publishes the new daily rate (typically before 11:00 AM NST)
  2. Our server fetches the updated rate via a scheduled task
  3. The new rate is stored and served to our website frontend
  4. The website displays the updated price, including a "Last Updated" timestamp
  5. Historical price records are stored in our database for the price history chart

On public holidays and bank holidays when FENEGOSIDA does not publish a new rate, the previous business day's rate remains displayed. This is clearly indicated on the website.

Purity Levels and Derived Rates

FENEGOSIDA publishes the 24K (Fine Gold / Tejabi) rate directly. Lower purity rates are calculated proportionally:

  • 24K Fine Gold (99.9%): Published directly by FENEGOSIDA
  • 22K Tejabi (91.67%): Published directly by FENEGOSIDA
  • 18K (75%): 24K rate × 0.75
  • 14K (58.3%): 24K rate × 0.583
  • Silver (Chandi): Published directly by FENEGOSIDA (per tola)

Historical Price Data

Our historical price records are compiled from FENEGOSIDA daily rates going back several years. Our price history chart and table show the actual FENEGOSIDA rate for each trading day. Gaps in the chart correspond to public holidays and market closures.

Calculator Methodology

Our Gold Calculator and other tools use the current FENEGOSIDA rate as the base price. Derived calculations:

  • Per gram: Per tola price ÷ 11.664 (grams per tola)
  • Per 10 grams: Per gram × 10
  • Making charges: User-defined percentage applied to the raw gold value; not a recommendation
  • Gold loan: Raw gold value × LTV ratio; banks' actual terms will vary

What We Do Not Source

  • Individual jeweller prices (these vary by dealer and are not regulated)
  • International spot prices in USD (available on international commodity sites)
  • Futures or forward prices
  • Prices from unofficial or unlicensed dealers

Reporting Errors

If you notice a price that appears incorrect or a data discrepancy, please contact us immediately via the Contact page. We take data accuracy seriously and will investigate promptly.
