What is Tejabi Gold (22K)?

Tejabi gold is the most widely traded gold type in Nepal, used almost exclusively for jewellery. With a purity of 91.67% (916 fineness), it contains 22 parts gold and 2 parts other metals — typically copper and silver — which give it strength and hardness suitable for everyday jewellery.

FENEGOSIDA sets the official Tejabi gold rate every morning based on the 24K price. The 22K price is always approximately 91.67% of the 24K Fine Gold rate.

22K Tejabi Gold vs 24K Fine Gold

  • Purity: 22K = 91.67% gold  |  24K = 99.9% gold
  • Price: 22K is cheaper per tola — saves roughly 8% vs 24K
  • Use: 22K is ideal for jewellery; 24K for investment bars and coins
  • Durability: 22K is harder and scratch-resistant; 24K is too soft for jewellery
  • Resale: Both are readily resold at Nepal gold shops at the FENEGOSIDA rate

How to Calculate 22K Gold Value

To calculate the value of 22K Tejabi gold:

  • Tola to NPR: Weight in tola × 22K per-tola rate
  • Grams to NPR: Weight in grams × 22K per-gram rate
  • Add making charges: Typically 8–15% of the gold value for jewellery

Buying 22K Gold Jewellery in Nepal — Tips

  • Always verify the hallmark stamp (916 for 22K) on the jewellery
  • Check the current FENEGOSIDA rate before negotiating the gold price component
  • Making charges (बनाउनी) are separate from the gold price — typically 8–15%
  • Ask for a bill showing gold weight, purity, and price breakdown
  • Prefer FENEGOSIDA-registered dealers for transparency and fair pricing
  • Consider buying plain gold bars/coins for investment, and ornaments separately

22K Tejabi Gold Price FAQ

Common questions about 22K Tejabi gold in Nepal

The current 22K Tejabi gold price per tola in Nepal is shown live at the top of this page. It is updated daily from FENEGOSIDA's official announcement and includes all applicable duties and taxes.
916 gold is another name for 22K gold. The "916" refers to its fineness — 916 parts out of 1000 are pure gold (91.6%). Jewellery pieces stamped "916" or "22K" are the same gold. This is the standard for most Nepal gold jewellery.
1 aana = 1/16 tola = 0.729 grams. To calculate the value of 1 aana of 22K gold, divide the per-tola rate by 16. Use our Gold Calculator for instant conversion from aana to NPR.
The FENEGOSIDA gold price already includes 13% VAT and 10% customs duty applied during import. When you buy jewellery from a shop, the gold price component reflects these taxes. Making charges may attract additional VAT depending on the jeweller.
