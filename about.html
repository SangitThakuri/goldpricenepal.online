About goldpricenepal.online

Nepal's trusted platform for live gold prices, charts, and calculators

Who We Are

goldpricenepal.online is an independent digital platform dedicated to providing Nepali consumers, investors, and jewellery buyers with accurate, up-to-date gold and silver price information. We believe everyone in Nepal deserves easy access to transparent gold market data without needing to call multiple shops or wait for morning newspapers.

Our website pulls live international gold spot prices, converts them using real-time USD/NPR exchange rates, and applies Nepal's applicable import duties and taxes to give you the most accurate indicative gold prices in Nepalese Rupees (NPR).

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower every Nepali with the gold market knowledge they need to make informed buying and selling decisions. Whether you are a first-time jewellery buyer, a seasoned investor, or a gold dealer looking for quick reference prices — goldpricenepal.online is built for you.

What We Offer

  • Live Gold Prices — Updated every 5 minutes from international markets
  • Multiple Purity Rates — 24K, 22K, 18K, and 14K gold in NPR
  • Multiple Units — Per tola (bhori), per gram, and per 10 grams
  • Silver Prices — Live silver rates in NPR alongside gold
  • Interactive Charts — Price trend charts for 7 days, 1 month, and 3 months
  • Gold Calculator — Calculate total gold price including making charges
  • Educational Content — Guides on buying gold, purity, and Nepal's gold market

How Prices Are Calculated

We source international gold spot prices (in USD per troy ounce) from metals market data providers and combine them with live USD to NPR exchange rates. We then apply Nepal's standard import duties (~10% customs duty and 13% VAT) along with typical dealer margins to arrive at indicative retail prices.

Please note: Our prices are indicative estimates. The official daily gold rate in Nepal is announced each morning by FENEGOSIDA (Federation of Nepal Gold and Silver Dealers' Association). We strongly recommend verifying with FENEGOSIDA or a registered dealer before making any purchase or sale.

Our Commitment to Transparency

We clearly label all prices as estimates and explain our calculation methodology. We do not receive commissions from gold dealers. Our content is independently created and maintained. We are committed to being a trustworthy, ad-supported free service for the Nepali community.
